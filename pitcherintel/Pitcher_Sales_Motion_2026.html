01
ACCOUNT SCORING & PRIORITIZATION
Score, Rank & Decide Where to Focus
Combine intent signals, ICP fit, first-party behavioral data, and call history to produce a single account priority score. This prevents BDRs from wasting time on wrong accounts and ensures the highest-probability accounts get attention first.
  • Nooks Prospect ingests signal data + CRM data → ML-driven account score
  • Accounts ranked into tiers: Tier 1 (immediate outreach), Tier 2 (this month), Tier 3 (nurture)
  • Explainable AI shows which specific signals drove the score
  • Scores refresh dynamically as new signals arrive — priority shifts in real time
  • Manager reviews Tier 1 weekly; BDR works Tier 1 daily
Output → Ranked account list with signal breakdown, ready for prospecting

02
PROSPECT IDENTIFICATION
Find the Right People at the Right Accounts
At each prioritized account, identify 10–20 specific personas to target. Multi-threaded outreach dramatically increases conversion rates — don't go single-threaded. Target a mix of Economic Buyer, Champion, and Blocker personas simultaneously.
  • Economic Buyers: VP/Head of Sales, CRO, Chief Commercial Officer
  • Champions: Sales Enablement Manager, RevOps Director, Head of Field Sales
  • Technical: VP Product / IT (for Catalyst module conversations)
  • Pharma-specific: Head of Commercial Excellence, Field Force Manager, Medical Affairs VP
  • Filter by: seniority, tenure (avoid recent hires <3mo), location — and keyword titles below
  • Validate: recent job changes, LinkedIn activity, content engagement

03
CONTACT ENRICHMENT
Get Verified Data — Mobile, Email, Context
Contact data decays at ~30% per year. Before any outreach, enrich every prospect with verified mobile numbers, direct dials, and work emails. Nooks Numbers runs waterfall enrichment across multiple providers automatically — only charges credits when a valid number is found.
  • Waterfall enrichment: query Cognism → Datagma → LeadIQ → ZoomInfo sequentially
  • Stop at first valid mobile number — cost-efficient, maximum coverage
  • Invalid/DNC numbers automatically flagged and removed from future campaigns
  • Enrich company data: firmographics, tech stack, recent news, competitor tech used
  • Job change signals: promoted or moved in last 90 days = high-priority trigger
  • Funding round alerts: Series B+ = expansion budget available
Output → Verified mobile + email + enriched profile ready for CRM import
Tools Used
Nooks Numbers Cognism Datagma LeadIQ ZoomInfo Lusha
✓ Actions in this step
1. Select all saved prospects from Stage 02 list
2. Run Nooks Numbers → waterfall enrichment in bulk
3. Review results: confirm mobile + work email found
4. Flag DNC / unverified → remove from campaign
5. Enrich company record: tech stack, recent news, competitor used
6. Mark enrichment complete → ready for CRM import
04
CRM IMPORT & HYGIENE
Load into HubSpot — Clean, Tagged, Assigned
Clean CRM data is the foundation of everything. Contacts enter HubSpot in a structured, deduplicated way with all relevant context tagged — signal source, account tier, persona type, and enrichment timestamp. A dirty CRM means wasted outreach and invisible pipeline.
  • One-click import from Nooks Prospect directly into HubSpot (deduplication built-in)
  • Auto-tag: signal source (G2/6Sense/TAL), account tier (1/2/3), persona type
  • Set lifecycle stage = Prospect; assign to BDR owner
  • Custom properties: account_temperature, competitor_used, enrichment_date, intent_signal
  • Company record: link contact → company, set deal association if relevant
  • Trigger HubSpot automation: new Tier 1 contact → notify BDR via Slack immediately
Output → HubSpot contact + company record, tagged, assigned, sequence-ready

05
CONTENT CREATION & PERSONALIZATION
Build the Message — Per Persona, Per Signal
Generic outreach dies in spam. Every message must be grounded in a specific signal, reference the prospect's reality, and make a relevant Pitcher angle land. AI accelerates content creation — but the BDR reviews and approves every message before it sends.
  • Nooks Prospect generates AI email draft using: intent signals + call history + buyer context
  • Pitcher Intel (Claude AI) supplements with: account research, competitive angle, industry pain
  • Per prospect: 3–5 email variations + LinkedIn message + cold call script
  • Personalization anchors: competitor they use (Seismic/Veeva/IQVIA), trigger event, role-specific pain
  • Pitcher angles: Catalyst AI App Builder, Virtual Coach roleplay, Seismic merger disruption
  • Message reviewed by BDR before sequence launch — AI assists, human approves
Output → Full message set per prospect: email thread, LinkedIn copy, call script

07
LIVE CONVERSATION — THE COLD CALL
The Moment That Matters Most
Nooks Dial handles the infrastructure — spam protection, parallel dialing, voicemail skip. The BDR focuses on the conversation. Real-time AI surfaces objection responses, competitor battlecards, and relevant context during the call — without the rep leaving the screen.
  • Before: Nooks surfaces account context, recent signals, call script, and prior call history
  • During: real-time transcription, AI prompts for objections, competitor responses surface automatically
  • Parallel dialing: dial multiple numbers, connect only when a live human picks up — 3× more conversations per hour
  • Spam protection: Nooks Numbers rotates caller IDs, manages carrier reputation
  • Virtual Salesfloor: manager can listen live, whisper coach, or run blitz rooms
  • Post-call: outcome, notes, and next action auto-logged to HubSpot — zero manual CRM work

08
MEETING BOOKED → AE HANDOFF
Convert the Meeting into a Qualified Opportunity
A booked meeting is not won pipeline. The BDR-to-AE handoff must be airtight — full context transfer, confirmed attendees, pre-call research, and a clear hypothesis for why Pitcher wins this account. A weak handoff loses meetings that were hard to book.
  • HubSpot deal created at Stage 0 (S0) immediately on meeting confirmation
  • AE receives: account intelligence summary, signal history, call notes, LinkedIn profile, competitor context
  • Pitcher Intel (Claude AI) generates account research brief: company pain, org structure, competitive landscape
  • Pre-meeting prep: AE reviews Virtual Coach roleplay scenarios for this specific persona/industry
  • BDR joins first 5 minutes of discovery call for warm introduction — reduces no-show rate
  • Post-meeting: AE updates deal stage, notes, next steps in HubSpot within 24h
Output → S0 deal in HubSpot, AE briefed, discovery call scheduled, Pitcher winning hypothesis defined
